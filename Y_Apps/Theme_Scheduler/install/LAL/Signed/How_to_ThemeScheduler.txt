
Theme Scheduler is a free theme scheduler application that allows automated theme schduling with timer, 
or automated theme changing according to active profile.

Just open the application by clicking its icon, and select "New" and then either "Profile change" or "Timed change" depending on which with you want to change 
the theme with. Then just and add the data for the schedule and click "Save".

When you have schedules stored in to the database, you can also use menu options for modifying and deleting them.

Note that to actually re-fresh the scheduling, you need to exit the application. Also each schedule should have two minutes time difference between 
them for for reliable operations. Also when exiting the application, anyw new/modified schedule should be at least two minutes in the future.


Note that this application does not support back upping.
